Visiting Library

Tours / Briefings

We welcome local and international delegations and individuals from educational institutions, research institutions, and libraries who are interested in learning more about NTU Library. We can offer tours and/or briefings with appropriate staff depending on your interests.

Please provide a brief description of your interest and preferred dates via our online enquiry form and we will contact you to make arrangements.
 

Visitor Pass

If you are not a member of NTU Library but would like one-time access to any of our libraries, you may request for a temporary visitor's pass. Please email to library@ntu.edu.sg.
